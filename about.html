  • The game is set in the Lands Between, a sprawling fantasy realm once ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal and blessed by the Erdtree and its divine power, the Elden Ring.
  • The Elden Ring, source of the Erdtree's grace, has been shattered. Its fragments, known as Great Runes, are held by demigods (Marika's children), whose ambition and corruption have plunged the world into chaos.
  • You play as a Tarnished, an exile called back to the Lands Between to collect the Great Runes, restore the Elden Ring, and become the Elden Lord
