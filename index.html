Elden Ring is an expansive action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, released in 2022. Directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, with worldbuilding contributions from fantasy novelist George R. R. Martin, it takes players into the mysterious and perilous Lands Between. As a Tarnished, you rise from exile to seek the power of the Elden Ring—a shattered force that once governed the world’s order. The game combines FromSoftware’s signature challenging combat with vast open-world exploration, filled with dungeons, towering castles, hidden secrets, and fearsome bosses. With freedom of choice in how to explore, fight, and shape your journey, Elden Ring offers a deeply immersive experience that challenges both skill and strategy while unraveling an epic tale of ruin, ambition, and destiny.
